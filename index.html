Our Story

A sanctuary above the sea

Raha — meaning comfort, ease, and pleasure in Swahili — is a private luxury villa perched on the edge of Zanzibar's turquoise coast. Born from a passion for authentic African elegance, every corner of Raha is designed to immerse you in the island's soul while enveloping you in effortless comfort.

From sunrise over the Indian Ocean to candlelit evenings under a canopy of stars, Villa Raha offers a stay that is as unforgettable as the island itself.

The Experience

Beyond the villa

Zanzibar is an island of spice, history, and infinite blue. From Raha, the entire island is yours to explore.

01

Spice Tours

Discover cloves, vanilla and cinnamon growing wild on guided plantation tours through the island's legendary spice farms.

02

Stone Town

Wander the UNESCO-listed Stone Town, a labyrinth of carved doors, ancient mosques, and vibrant spice markets.

03

Snorkeling & Diving

The Indian Ocean teems with reef fish, dolphins, and sea turtles. Private boat trips arranged door-to-door.

04

Sunset Dhow Cruises

Sail traditional Swahili wooden dhows as the sky turns amber — one of Zanzibar's most iconic experiences.
